Job Specifications


Job Summary

We are looking for a junior web developer to join our enthusiastic team in our Swansea Office. You will be assisting with the programming, design and testing of the website. You will be working with other junior web developed whilst taking direction from the senior developers.

To be able to complete this job properly, you will need to possess an excelent understanding of web-design and the skills that go with it as well as a great attention to detail. You should also be able to work well under pressure in a highly technical environment.



Job Requirements

  • A bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent qualification in a similar field.
  • Experience in HTML, CSS, JQuery, Python, JSON and Ajax.
  • A good understanding of the web-design and development principles.
  • Good project management skills.
  • Critical thinking skills.
  • An in-depth knowledge of software prototyping and UX/UI design tools.
  • You will also be required to provide a portfolio of recent prjects that you have worked on.

Job Responsibilities

  • Assisting with all aspects of the design and development process.
  • Assisting with maintenance and testing of all aspects of deployed websites.
  • Contributing ideas to new technology applications such as digital storage and mobile technology.
  • Collaborating with developers to implement new features.
  • Keep up-to-date with the latest advancements in the technology and programming fields.
  • Converting video, audio, images and other components into compatible web formats.
  • Using feedback from users to identify and correct problems with depolyed websites.

Employee Benefits

  • Starting salary: £27,000
  • Holidays: 4 weeks paid holiday, excluding bank holidays.
  • Discounted gym membership with Simply Gym.
  • 28 weeks sick pay.
  • Desk decoration allowance of up to £100 to decorate your desk with.
  • Flexible working including work from home availability.
  • Company provided bicycle.
  • Company provided counsellors.
  • Pension contribution matching.
  • Childcare support.
  • Life Insurance scheme.
  • Other benefits available.

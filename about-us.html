About Us

Hot Beans Web is a web development company based primarily in Swansea in Wales with offices in Cardiff, Manchester and London. The company was founded in March of 2009 and has been thriving ever since. We opened our first office in Swansea in August of 2009. We then opened our second office in the Cardiff Bay area in October 2012. We shortly followed this by opening our thrid office in Manchetster in January 2013. We opened our final officein London in 2015. We currently have over 200 employees across the 4 offices with the majority of them in Swansea. We have been involved with websites such as the Government Website, the Hwb website and the Ministry of Defence Gateway as well as the BBC website and the Gower College Swansea website to name just a few.
